Break Time, 2009

3-channel video loop, silent.
16:9, 16.50 minutes.

A 3-channel installation featuring office workers on their smoking break. The sampled and choreographed sequences follow the slow and arbitrary movements of the distant and lonely smokers and focus on the act of passing time.
